Hola! Soy Irmarie!

For a long time I used to swim competitively.

I love pets, especially dogs.

Most of my days I perform research related to accessibility and cultural sensitivity in LIS and Archival Systems.

My work is inspired by my life expereinces as an afro-caribbean women living in-between places.

I usually find myself thinking about how things are conected, how is the best way to organize stuff, and how things can be accesible for my own community.
